Our services

We offer a full suite of services to help businesses and individuals protect themselves against online threats. Our cybersecurity company specializes in online data protection and security. Here is a more detailed description of our services

Security audit

Our security audit service helps identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in computer systems. We provide a detailed report with recommendations to improve security. Our security audits include:

  • Vulnerability analysis: our IT security team analyzes IT systems to detect vulnerabilities that could be exploited by attackers.
  • Risk analysis: we assess the risks associated with IT systems and propose security measures to reduce them.
  • Analysis of security policies: we review the company's security policies to ensure that they comply with security standards.
  • Security controls analysis: we evaluate the security controls in place to ensure that they are effective and adapted to the needs of the company.

Security management

Our IT security management service is designed to ensure optimal protection against online attacks. We offer comprehensive IT security management, including the implementation of security measures, 24/7 monitoring, and security incident response. Our security management services include:

  • The design and implementation of security policies: we help companies to implement security policies to protect their data online.
  • Computer systems monitoring: our team constantly monitors computer systems to detect threats in real time.
  • Detection and response to security incidents: we are available 24/7 to respond to security incidents and take measures to counter them.
  • Security patch management: we take care of updating IT systems to ensure that the latest security patches are installed.
  • Identity and access management: we help companies implement identity and access control policies to protect their data online.

Safety awareness

We offer training to educate employees on good online security practices. We adapt our training to the needs of each company and provide practical advice to improve security. Our security awareness training includes:

  • Employee training on online security best practices: We provide practical advice to help employees recognize and avoid online threats.
  • Employee training on password management: we help employees create strong passwords and protect them against online attacks.
  • Employee training on handling sensitive data: we help employees understand how to protect sensitive company data.
  • Training employees on the risks associated with the use of personal devices in the company: we help employees understand the risks associated with the use of personal devices in the company and put in place security measures to protect the company data.

Real-time monitoring

Our real-time monitoring service helps detect threats in real time and take action to counter them. We use advanced monitoring tools to detect threats and we have a dedicated real-time monitoring team. Our real-time monitoring services include:

  • Computer systems monitoring: we monitor computer systems to detect threats in real time.
  • Detection and response to security incidents: we are available 24/7 to respond to security incidents and take measures to counter them.
  • Security patch management: we take care of updating IT systems to ensure that the latest security patches are installed.

Incident response

Our incident response service helps minimize damage in the event of a security incident. Our team is available 24/7 to respond to security incidents and take action to counter them. Our incident response services include:

  • Security alert management: We monitor security alerts to detect security incidents.
  • Security Incident Response: We are available 24/7 to respond to security incidents and take action to counter them.
  • Security patch management: we take care of updating IT systems to ensure that the latest security patches are installed.

We are also able to provide customized security services to meet the specific needs of each business or individual. We work closely with our clients to understand their cybersecurity needs and offer tailored solutions.
